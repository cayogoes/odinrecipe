How to make a Brigadeiro:

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 cup of chocolate powder
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • Chocolate sprinkles (for decoration)

How to prepare:

  1. Heat the condensed milk in a saucepan until it thickens.
  2. Add the chocolate powder and butter, stirring until well combined.
  3. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  4. Roll the mixture into small balls and coat them with chocolate sprinkles.
