How to make a Coxinha:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of shredded chicken
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Breadcrumbs (for coating)
  • Oil (for frying)

How to prepare:

  1. In a saucepan, combine the chicken broth, milk, butter, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil.
  2. Gradually add the flour, stirring constantly until a dough forms and pulls away from the sides of the pan.
  3. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  4. Take a small portion of the dough, flatten it in your hand, and place a spoonful of shredded chicken in the center. Fold the dough around the filling and shape it into a teardrop.
  5. Coat the shaped coxinhas in breadcrumbs.
  6. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot and fry the coxinhas until golden brown. Drain on paper towels before serving.
