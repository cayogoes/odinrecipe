How to make a Feijoada:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of dried beans
  • 2 cups of pork shoulder
  • 1 cup of beef brisket
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

How to prepare:

  1. Rinse the dried beans and soak them in water for at least 4 hours.
  2. Cook the beans in a large pot with enough water to cover them. Add the pork and beef, along with the onion, garlic, and bay leaves. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Simmer for about 2 hours or until the meat is tender and the beans are cooked through.
  4. Serve hot with rice, collard greens, and orange slices.
